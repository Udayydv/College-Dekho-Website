KIIT Bhubaneshwar

Information

Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology (KIIT), formerly KIIT University, is a private deemed university located in Bhubaneswar, Odisha, India. It primarily emphasizes on higher education and research in engineering and science. It offers 34 undergraduate, 32 postgraduate, 10 integrated, 11 Ph.D and 7 postdoctoral research programmes in the fields of science and engineering, medical science, management, law, film and media, humanities and yoga and sports. The admissions for B.Tech. programmes are purely based on merit as per the results in KIITEE exam. It was founded in 1992 as Industrial Training Institute in Bhubaneswar with a seed revenue of ₹5000 (US$66.8). In 2017 it was renamed back as Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology following the UGC request to drop "University" from the name for all the institutes granted the status of 'Deemed to be Universities'. It is part of KIIT Society which is estimated to be worth ₹10,000 Crores (US$1.3 billion) as of 2020. It was declared as deemed university in 2004 by the Ministry of HRD, Government of India and also was conferred with Category A status in 2014 by the Government of India. In 2019 KIIT was recognised as Institute of Eminence by the Ministry of HRD, Government of India. It has been granted Tier-1 accrediation by the National Board of Accreditation. ARIIA 2021 ranked KIIT as India's best private university in innovation and self-financing

Admissions

KIIT University offers B.Tech and B.Arch programs under KIIT School of Engineering in various disciplines. The minimum eligibility criteria for B.Tech is 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics/ Biology with 60% marks in aggregate. For B.Arch program, 10+2 (60%) with Mathematics as the main subject is mandatory. KIIT conducts KIITEE to offer admission in B.Tech course followed by counselling. B.Arch admission is based on a valid score in NATA. Final admission is confirmed through counselling. KIIT also offers admission to B.Tech+M.Tech (Integrated)/ B.Tech+MBA (Dual Degree) and B.Tech (Lateral Entry) on the basis of performance in KIITEE followed by coiunselling.

Keypoints

  • For B.Tech-M.Tech (Dual Degree) programs, candidates who have passed 10+2 in 2018, 2019 or appearing are eligible to apply.
  • For B.Tech-M.Tech (Dual Degree) programs, the candidates should have born on or after July 01, 1998.
  • KIITEE is a Computer Based Test (CBT) test with Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs). Each correct answer will give you four marks. One mark will be deducted for every wrong answer.

Documents required at the time of KIITEE Counselling :-

  • Admit card/ Rank card
  • 10th & 12th pass certificate
  • GATE Scorecard (if applicable)
  • DD of INR 75,000 which includes a registration fee of INR 10,000 in favor of ‘KIIT” payable at Bhubaneshwar.

Scholarships

Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology (KIIT) University Scholarships are given to students under various categories. Among all the scholarships, some famous scholarships or Tuition Fee Waiver Scheme (TFW) are:

  1. Pradyumna Bal Memorial Scholarship :-

  The scholarship is instituted in the memory of Late Pradyumna Kishore Bal, Founder President of KIIT. Every year two poor and meritorious students are provided completely free education under this scholarship scheme.

  2. KIIT Poverty-Cum-Merit Scholarship :-

  KIIT has instituted Poverty-cum-Merit Scholarship for the poor and needy students. The amount and period of scholarship are decided based on the need of the selected candidates.

  3. KIIT Merit Scholarship :-

  KIIT Merit Scholarship is provided to University toppers (1st, 2nd & 3rd ) of each semester for six months.

  4. KIITEE Merit Scholarship :-

  KIIT provides free education to the topper of KIITEE.
