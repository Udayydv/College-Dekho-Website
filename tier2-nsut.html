NSUT

Information

NSUT (Netaji Subhas University of Technology, Delhi), formerly known as the NSIT Delhi is one of the popular technological universities in India. Graduates from the institute can easily bag the job offers ranging from INR 4 LPA to INR 1.25 Cr., at various national and international companies, MNCs, Software Firms, and others. Some of the major recruiters of NSIT Delhi include Amazon India, KPMG, Maruti Suzuki, Reliance Jio, and many others.

NSIT Quick Facts:

  • NSIT Delhi offers several incentives and financial assistance schemes for its UG, PG, and Ph.D. students, Faculty, Officers, and Staff of the NSUT.

  • The institute has a fully residential campus spread over 145 acres of land consisting of all the necessary and modern amenities for its students and staff including Medical Facility (Physiotherapy, Homeopathy), Power backup, Incubator for students, alumni, and faculty, yoga & meditation Centre, Gym facility, University management system, university networking system, internet facilities, broadband facilities, hostels, and many others.

  • NSIT Delhi has a large alumni network of over 5000 graduate members and prominent alumni who are serving their best leadership roles and positions at the top and reputed firms across the globe since their graduation.

  • The faculty members of NSIT Delhi are highly skilled and qualified professionals to provide the appropriate guidance and learning experience to the students of the institute. Most of the faculty members are postgraduates while some of them are ex-civil professionals and Ph.D. holders.

  • NSIT Delhi has a placement that organizes and manages on-campus placement drives and professional training sessions for the students. The highest salary package recorded by the institute was INR 1.25 crores offered to a B.E. graduate.

Admissions

NSUT B.Tech Admission 2022 NSUT offers 10 specializations under the B.Tech course. To apply, a candidate should have cleared 10+2 with Physics, Mathematics and Chemistry/Biotechnology with 75% marks or should feature among the top 20 percentile. For selection, the candidate has to take the JEE- Main.

NSIT B.Tech Eligibility Criteria

  • • 10+2 in with Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics/ Biotechnology

  • • 75% marks aggregate (65% for SC/ST categories) or top 20 percentile

NSIT B.Tech Selection Criteria

  • Selection to the B.Tech program at NSIT Delhi is made on the basis of the merits/ranks secured by the students in the JEE Main entrance exam.

  • However, the final selection and seat allotment will be done after the successful completion of the JAC Delhi Counselling Process.
