IIT Madras

Information

IIT Madras is the best engineering institute among all the IITs in India. For the 6th consecutive year, IIT Madras is ranked at 1st in Engineering category of NIRF. IIT Madras has 17 departments, 14 research centres, and a Continuing Education Programs Centre (CEP). All of the centres offer 121 courses in Engineering, Science, Humanities, and Management. IIT Madras has signed MOUs with a number of foreign universities for student exchange programs. As per the MoUs signed, students pursuing UG, PG, and Dual Degree programs at the institute spend a semester abroad for broad exposure. In addition, the institute also offers Joint Doctoral Programs (JDPs) in collaboration with 17 foreign universities. The institute also provides an AICTE-sponsored Quality Improvement Program (QIP). Out of the 121 courses offered by IIT Madras, BTech and Online Degree program has the highest popularity. BTech is the flagship program of the institute. Admission to the course is done on the basis of rank obtained in the JEE Advanced exam. The minimum rank required for the IIT Madras BTech program is 85 to 4000. The annual fee for the BTech program is INR 2.05 Lacs. IIT Madras Online Degree is India's first online program in Data Science and Programming. Admission to the degree program is based on Qualifier exam score. The candidates appearing for the Qualifier exams are required to score aggregate 50% marks in the exam. The fees for the online degree program range from INR 50k to 1 Lac. Also, IIT Madras’s placement cell has a strong relationship with the industries. The 2022 placement drive is ongoing. Till now 380 recruiters participated. 1199 offers have been made so far. Students also received 231 PPOs.

Admissions

The Indian Institute of Technology, Madras offers the B.Tech program across 10 different specializations in the minimum duration of 4 years via regular mode. Also, the institute charges a total fee of INR 2.84 Lakhs for its B.Tech program. Some of the popular specializations under IIT Madras B.Tech program include Aerospace, Computer Science &, Chemical, and Mechanical Engineering. However, the other basic eligibility and selection details for the program are mentioned below.

IIT Madras B.Tech Eligibility Criteria

Candidates applying for IIT Bombay BTech & Integrated B.Tech program must have:

  • Applicants should have completed his/her 10+2 from a recognized board of education with Physics, Chemistry, English, and Mathematics as main subjects.

  • Also, the applicants should have secured at least 60% marks in aggregate in the 10+2 level or equivalent qualifying examination.

IIT Madras B.Tech Selection Criteria

  • Considering the 12th exam results, JEE Main and JEE Advanced/ AAT score, a merit list shall be made category-wise, listing All India Rank (AIR) of candidates.

  • Candidates have to register for JoSaa counselling online at the official JoSSA website. Choice filling and locking is mandatory and it is to be done by the candidate only. In decreasing the order of preference, branch choices are to be filled.

B.Tech admissions in IITs is based on JEE Advanced exam scores. Candidates who secure minimum qualifying marks in the JEE Main examination are eligible to appear for the respected examination. The minimum qualifying marks for Unreserved category (General category) is set for 87.992241 and the maximum mark is 100. Those who have secured the minimum cutoff marks and are ranked under 2.5 lakh can appear for JEE Advanced 2021.

Documents required

  • A scanned clear signature of the candidate in JPG/JPEG format (size between 4 kb – 30 kb).

  • A valid e-mail ID.

  • An offer of admission letter

  • Birth Certificate issued by Local Government Body

  • Community Certificate

  • Certificate for EWS as per JEE format

  • Certificate in the case of PwD candidates as per JEE format

  • Admit Card of JEE (Advance)
