IIT Kharagpur

Information

IIT Kharagpur is one of the top rated Engineering college located in India. Currently, the college is ranked 5th in NIRF 2022 ranking in the engineering discipline. The core areas of education and research of the institute are Engineering, Architecture, Management, Law, and Humanities. IIT Kharagpur offers 117 courses in 5 areas. IIT Kharagpur campus has a campus space of 2100, which is the largest among all IITs. The campus has 19 academic departments, 8 multidisciplinary centres/schools, and 13 schools of excellence. In addition, there are more than 25 central research units, and 24 on-campus hostels for students, research scholars, and faculty members. The flagship program of the institute is the BTech. The program is specialized in 15 streams and has an intake capacity of 818 seats. To get admission to the flagship program, candidates are required to score a rank in the top 30 percentile in the JEE exam. IIT Kharagpur cutoff for the BTech admission is 200-7000 rank in the JEE Advanced. The per semester fee for the program is INR 1.34 Lacs. The fee is only chargeable to general category students. SC/ ST/ PwD/ candidates are exempted from any sort of fee payment. The institute is well-known for its Law School, Rajiv Gandhi School of Intellectual Property (RGSOIPL). IIT Kharagpur is the only IIT that offers Law courses. The school offers three programs; LLB, LLM, and PhD. The LLB program of the School is affiliated with the Bar Council of India. Several of the faculty members at IIT Kharagpur Law school have international publications. The faculty members have received numerous awards as well, and are members of both national and international organizations. The school conducts workshops, clinical, legal courses, courtroom exercises to provide in-house training to the students. Check IIT Kharagpur Law Admission 2023 For the placements 2021-22, IIT Kharagpur has set a record of achieving 1600 placement offers in the first phase. As per the reports, 20 students received salary packages of more than INR 1 CPA for the domestic placements. For the international placements, the salary package went up to INR 2.4 CPA and INR 2 CPA, received by two students. The institute received a total of 35 international offers from companies such as Google, Microsoft, Micron Technology, Uber, Honeywell, Excel.

IIT Kharagpur B.Tech Eligibility Criteria

Candidates applying for IIT Kharagpur BTech & Integrated B.Tech program must have:

  • Completed 10+2 with a JEE score within the cut-offs ranges of the institute.

  • The applicant should be of a minimum of 17 years.

IIT Kharagpur B.Tech Selection Criteria

  • Considering the 12th exam results, JEE Main and JEE Advanced/ AAT score, a merit list shall be made category-wise, listing All India Rank (AIR) of candidates.

  • Candidates have to register for JoSaa counselling online at the official JoSSA website. Choice filling and locking is mandatory and it is to be done by the candidate only. In decreasing the order of preference, branch choices are to be filled.

B.Tech admissions in IITs is based on JEE Advanced exam scores. Candidates who secure minimum qualifying marks in the JEE Main examination are eligible to appear for the respected examination. The minimum qualifying marks for Unreserved category (General category) is set for 87.992241 and the maximum mark is 100. Those who have secured the minimum cutoff marks and are ranked under 2.5 lakh can appear for JEE Advanced 2021.

Scholarships

IIT Kharagpur offers an array of scholarships for its students pursuing UG & PG Programs on a full-time basis. The faculty of the institute also offers research fundings & fellowships to meritorious students. Apart from scholarships, IIT KGP offers financial assistance to meet the expenses of accommodation, research, entrepreneurship, and international travel for various competitions. The scholarships offered by IIT Kharagpur have been broadly divided into many categories and some of them are mentioned below.

  Merit Cum Means Scholarship (Institute Scholarship) :-

The Merit Cum Means Scholarship is awarded from the institute's funds to more than 1000 students on the basis of merits every year. The rules for the Merit Cum Means Scholarship are as follows.

  • Any student enrolled in any program offered by IIT Kharagpur are eligible for merit cum means scholarships except the students belonging from the SC/ST categories.

  • However, there's a certain limit for the Merit Cum Means Scholarship as it is not offered to more than 25% of the students enrolled in the UG & dual degree programs at IIT Kharagpur.

  • Also, a student is required to maintain a minimum GPA of 7.00 for subsequent renewals of the Merit Cum Means Scholarship in the two consecutive semesters.

  Private Scholarships :-

Apart from the Merit Cum Means Scholarships, the institute also offers various private scholarships to its students

  • B.P. Poddar Scholarships:

    • The B.P. Poddar Scholarship is awarded to the best eligible students of the B.Tech/, B.Arch or Dual Degree Program securing the highest CGPA at the end of the semester.

    • The award of the Scholarship is INR 1000 per month, which is payable for the consecutive 10 months.

  • Kumud Manorama Scholarship:

    • The Kumud Manorama Scholarship is awarded to the 3rd year students of the B.Tech program and dual degrees in Mechanical Engineering as per the academic performances of the students.

    • A sum of INR 1000 is awarded per month in the Scholarship for the 10 months.

  • K.K. Agarwal Memorial Scholarship:

    • The K.K. Agarwal Memorial Scholarship is awarded to the best eligible students of the final year of B.Tech/ Dual Degree programs in Agriculture and Food Engineering.

    • The students receive a sum of INR 400 per month under this scheme of scholarship for consecutive 10 months.

  • Madan Sundar Sahu Lal Memorial Scholarship:

    • The Scholarship is granted to the students of the 2nd or final year enrolled in the B.Tech or Dual degree programs on the basis of CGPA scored by the student in the 2nd semester of the program.

    • Also, the students applying for Madan Sundar Sahu Lal Memorial Scholarship should have an annual family income of less than INR 2.5 lakhs.

    • An amount of INR 2100 is given per month to the students as an award of the scholarship.

There are also other several options of financial assistance offered at IIT KGP apart from the above-mentioned scholarships that can be availed by the students to meet their tuition fee expenses
