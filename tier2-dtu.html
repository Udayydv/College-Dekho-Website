DTU

Information

DTU is one of the oldest Engineering institutes in India. Established as Delhi Polytechnic institute, DTU became first engineering college of Delhi. The university is a part of TIFAC COREs (Technology Information, Forecasting & Assessment Council's Centres of Relevance & Excellence) in Fiber Optics and Optical Communication. TIFAC COREs is an initiative of Govt of India which insists on industry and academia collaborations for research and development. DTU Delhi offers more than 90 courses in Engineering, Design, Science, and Management. The 90 courses offered include 5 UG, 3 PG, and a PhD degree. The majority of the candidates seeking admission to the university opt for the BTech program. DTU BTech is the flagship program of the institute offered in 16 specializations. Selection to the program is based on JEE Main rank and JAC Delhi Counselling. DTU Cutoff for the program goes up to 1312 rank. The total fees for the program is INR 8.44 Lacs. Delhi Technical University is also famous for its management courses. For this purpose, the university has introduced the University School of Management and Entrepreneurship (USME). The campus is popularly known as DTU East Delhi Campus, due to its location. DTU, USME currently offers MBA, MBA business analytics, BBA, and BA in Economics. Admission to PG courses is offered on the basis of CAT percentile, whereas, UG course admissions are merit-based. Earlier, the DTU management programs were offered only through Delhi School of Management (DSM, DTU). The public business school was established under DTU Delhi and offers EMBA, MBA, and PhD programs in management.

Admissions

DTU B.Tech Admission 2022 Delhi Technological University (DTU) offers a full-time B.Tech course in 15 specializations. The minimum eligibility for admission is 10+2 (PCM) with at least 60% marks in aggregate from a recognized board of education. However, final admission is based on the score in JEE Main Followed by Joint Admission Counselling (JAC). The university also accepts applications for B.Tech lateral entry admissions from the best eligible candidates. However, the NRI/ PIO/ Foreign Nationals candidates can take admission through DASA (Direct Admission of Students’ Abroad). JEE Main ranks shall be considered for the admission of such candidates.

Keypoints :-

  • 10% of marks are relaxed for SC/ST category candidates and 5% for OBC candidates.

  • 10% relaxation of marks is also done for PwD candidates and under Defense, category 5% marks are relaxed.

  • DTU B.Tech Application Form can be filled online by visiting the official website.

  • Counseling fee (INR 1400) can be paid through Challan or Net Banking.

  • B.Tech (Evening) admission is made under two categories, i.e., Direct and Sponsored.

  • For B.Tech (Evening) program, the duly filled application form along with the application fee of INR 1000 in the form of DD drawn in favor of ‘Registrar, Delhi Technological University payable at Delhi should reach to Deputy Coordinator (Admin), DTU.

  • 85% of seats are reserved for those candidates who have passed their qualifying examination from the school located in Delhi or NCT of Delhi.

  • 15% of seats are reserved for the candidates belonging to the school located outside Delhi.

  • One seat in each course is reserved for Kashmiri Migrants (KM) and Single Girl Child (SG) who are residing in the NCT of Delhi.

  • One seat over and above the sanctioned intake is reserved for State/ Central Board Toppers.
