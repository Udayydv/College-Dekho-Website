MNIT

Information

Malaviya National Institute of Technology (MNIT) is an MHRD and AICTE approved University established in 1963. MNIT secured 46th position in Engineering as released by NIRF 2022. MNIT Jaipur has 16 departments and allied centres for Architecture, Engineering, and Management. MNIT Jaipur charges a fee of INR 3.22 Lacs for its flagship program, BTech. Fees for other courses ranges between INR 2.50 Lacs to INR 3.35 Lacs. MNIT Jaipur provides placement facilities to students with highest salary package upto INR 60 LPA. Average CTC offered at the institute is INR 10 LPA. The list of top recruiters at MNIT Jaipur includes Deloitte, DE Shaw, Capgemini, Cognizant, Apple, Goldman Sachs etc.
