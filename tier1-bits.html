BITS

Information

BITS Pilani is a private deemed university for higher education and research in Engineering. The university is among the first six institutes to be awarded ‘Institute of Eminence’ status. BITS Pilani is the only Indian private university featured in the top 200 universities in Asia by QS Asia University Rankings 2022. Birla Institute of Technology has campuses in Pilani, Goa, Hyderabad, Dubai, and Mumbai. With BITS Pilani in Dubai, the institute has become the first internationally deemed university. Along with the 5 established campuses, the university has 15 academic departments in Science, Engineering, Management, and Research. The institute also has 9 varied centres for Software Development, Robotics, Women Studies, and Desert Development Technologies. Much like other universities, BITS Pilani has a three tier academic structure- First degrees, Master’s degrees, and Doctoral degrees. Through these degrees, the university offers more than 35 courses like BE/BTech, BPharm, MTech, MPharm, MBA, MSc, and PhD. BITS Pilani conducts its own examination for admission to these programs. The deemed university conducts BITSAT exam for first degree programs and BITS HD for Master’ degree programs. The major degree program at BITS Pilani is the BTech. Admission to the program is done through the BITSAT entrance exam. BITS Pilani cutoff required for the BTech program is 245-378. The fees for the 4 year BITS Pilani BTech program is INR 18.98 Lacs. BITS Pilani also offers off-campus Work Integrated Learning Programs (WILP). The programs are designed for working professionals and organizations to focus on industry practices. The value of BITS Pilani WILP certificate is equivalent to regular learning programs as all the courses are approved by UGC. The courses offered cover areas such as Data Science, Data Engineering, and Advanced Analytics. BITS Pilani WILP offers BTech, MTech, MBA, MSc, PG Diploma, and PG Certificate programs in more than 30 specializations. Also, BITS Pilani is conducting 2022 placements and till date, 357 students have secured full time offers and 287 students have received Pre-placement offers. The highest CTC stood at INR 1.33 CPA and the average CTC at INR 28.99 LPA.

Admissions

Birla Institute of Technology and Science Admission Test (BITSAT) is a national-level entrance exam conducted by BITS Pilani itself. The examination is held every year to select students for BE, BPharm, and MSc courses. On an average, 3 lacs candidates every year appear for this exam in 50 centres. Candidates who have passes Class 12 with 75% aggregate marks in PCM with 60% in each subject are eligible to appear for this test.
