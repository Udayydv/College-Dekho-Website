IIT Delhi

Information

IIT Delhi is an autonomous public research institute, located in New Delhi. It is ranked 2nd among the top engineering colleges in India by NIRF. The institute has secured 174th place in the QS World University Rankings 2023. Indian Institute of Technology Delhi, popularly known as IIT Delhi, is a public research and engineering institute, established in 1961. The institute has its main campus located at Hauz Khas in Delhi, India. In addition, it has two extension campuses, one in Sonepat, and one in Jhajjar. It is one of the oldest IITs in India and has been declared as an “Institute of Eminence” by the government of India. IIT Delhi is best known for its wide variety of engineering programs and outstanding placement records. The college has been consistently ranked #2 among the top engineering colleges in India. For the flagship B.Tech program, the institute has always been one of the top preferences among all IITs. The institute charges an annual fee of INR 8.3 Lakhs for the same. Candidates who have cleared JEE Advanced examination and have secured an all India rank (AIR) of 1-100 are more likely to get admission to the IIT Delhi B.Tech program. Apart from the flagship program, IIT Delhi offers more than 100 courses under UG, PG, Dual, and Doctoral degrees. Additionally, the institute offers online certificate programs in the fields of Digital Marketing, Product Development, and Management. Check IIT Delhi Courses & Fees IIT Delhi placement stats have significantly increased in the past years. During the 2021-22 placement drive, the institute witnessed a 45% increase in the number of placement offers. The highest salary package stood at INR 2 CPA (International) and INR 1.25 CPA (Domestic). Some of the top recruiters at IIT Delhi are Microsoft, Intel, Oracle, Tata Projects, EXL Services, ICICI Bank, Goldman Sachs, HCL, etc.

IIT Delhi B.Tech Eligibility Criteria

Candidates applying for IIT Delhi BTech & Integrated B.Tech program must have:

  • Completed 10+2 with a JEE score within the cut-offs ranges of the institute.

  • The applicant should be of a minimum of 17 years.

IIT Delhi B.Tech Selection Criteria

  • Considering the 12th exam results, JEE Main and JEE Advanced/ AAT score, a merit list shall be made category-wise, listing All India Rank (AIR) of candidates.

  • Candidates have to register for JoSaa counselling online at the official JoSSA website. Choice filling and locking is mandatory and it is to be done by the candidate only. In decreasing the order of preference, branch choices are to be filled.

B.Tech admissions in IITs is based on JEE Advanced exam scores. Candidates who secure minimum qualifying marks in the JEE Main examination are eligible to appear for the respected examination. The minimum qualifying marks for Unreserved category (General category) is set for 87.992241 and the maximum mark is 100. Those who have secured the minimum cutoff marks and are ranked under 2.5 lakh can appear for JEE Advanced 2021.

Scholarships

Students enrolled across various courses at IIT Delhi are offered several scholarship schemes and financial assistance to lessen their financial burden and to cover academic and other expenses during the entire program. The institute offers both external and internal scholarships to its students based on their qualifications and scores. However, a student needs to satisfy minimum eligibility criteria to avail the facility of scholarship awards and grants. Some of the popular scholarship schemes offered by IIT Delhi are as follows:

  Merit Cum Means Scholarship (Institute Scholarship) :-

  • The MCM (Merit Cum Means) Scholarships are offered to the students in the Undergraduate Programs of Engineering & Technology or M.Sc Programs.

  • Only 25% of the students are sanctioned to get this scholarship as per the institute's rules.

  • Students of IIT Delhi whose annual family income or parent's gross income is up to INR 4.5 lakhs.

  • An award of INR 1000 is awarded per month to the students belonging to the GEN/OBC/EWS categories and the recipients are exempted from paying the tuition fee.

  • The tuition fee is exempted to all the students of the SC/ ST/ PwD category.

  Research Assistance :-

  • Research Assistance is provided to the students who are enrolled in the Ph.D. or research programs at IIT Delhi and are willing to work for the department.

  • Any student enrolled in the research-based programs on a full-time basis at IIT Delhi.

  • A minimum of INR 15,000 is awarded per month to students who can work about 20 hours a week.

  • For the students enrolled in MS Research Programme, a sum of INR 24,000 is awarded per month to work 8 hours a week.
