NIT Durgapur

Information

NIT Durgapur is ranked 34 by the NIRF 2022 in the Engineering category. The institute is among the eight colleges established under the co-operative ventures between Govt of India and West Bengal. NIT Durgapur is the first institute in Eastern India selected as the Lead Institute under the Technical Education Quality Improvement Program (TEQIP). The institute has 16 National and 17 International collaborations for student exchange programs, internships, and workshops. Currently, the institute has 14 departments, 5 Centre of Excellence, 224 faculty members, and 4000 students enrolled. NIT Durgapur offers 4 BTech programs, 19 MTech programs, 3 MSc programs, 1 MCA, 1 MBA, and 1 PhD program in spanning engineering, computer applications, sciences, and management. With primary focus on engineering, the two major programs of the institute are BTech and MTech. Admission to the BTech program is based on JEE Main rank, whereas, MTech admissions require GATE scores. Candidates seeking admission to these courses must score a rank between 4400-40000 in JEE Main exam for the BTech program. For the MTech program, the desirable GATE rank is between 400-800. NIT Durgapur rankings have been improved a lot over the years. The institute is at present, ranked 6th among all the NITs. Earlier, the institute was ranked 8th in 2020. The institute has moved up in the Engineering category as well. For the year 2021, NIT Durgapur is ranked 29 whereas for 2020 the institute stood at 47.Similarly, the highest CTC at NIT Durgapur has also increased. From being INR 44 LPA in 2021, the CTC has now reached up to INR 51 LPA in Placements 2023. During Placements 2022, a total of 560+ students has been placed so far in the BTech program.

NIT Durgapur B.Tech Eligibility Criteria

  • Candidates seeking admission in B.Tech at NIT must have passed their 10+2 from any recognized board of education in PCM.

  • A candidate must have scored at least 60% of marks in aggregate and must be eligible to give JEE Main exam.

NIT Durgapur B.Tech Selection Criteria

The selection to B.Tech programme at NIT is made on the basis of merit scored in JEE Main by the candidate.

  • The seat allotment takes place after the successful completion of JoSAA counseling process.
