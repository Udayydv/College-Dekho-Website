IIITL

Information

IIIT Lucknow is among the 20 IIITs that have been established under the Public-Private Partnership (PPP) Scheme by the Government of India. IIITL is recognized as an Institute of National Importance by the Act of Parliament. ute achieved 100% placements. The highest salary package stood at INR 1.2 CPA which was received by B.Tech students. The average salary of the B.Tech students stood at INR 25.86 LPA & average salary of the M.Tech students stood at INR 16.26 LPA. Some of the prominent recruiters of IIIT Lucknow are American Express, BYJU’s, Intel, Cognizant, AMD, Big Basket, Accenture, Deloitte, Darwin Box etc.

Admissions

The institute offers a 4 years long B.Tech program in 3 different specializations namely, Information Technology, Computer Science and Computer Science & Artificial Intelligence. The institute has a total intake of 80 students in each specialization. Admission are offered through JoSAA/CSAB counselling on the basis of JEE Main rank.

IIIT Lucknow B.Tech Eligibility Criteria

  • Candidates applying to the B.Tech course should have passed Class 12th or any equivalent examination with Physics, Mathematics and Chemistry/ Biotechnology or Biology as compulsory subjects and with minimum aggregate marks of 45% (40% for reserved category) from a recognized board.

IIIT Lucknow B.Tech Selection Criteria

  • Admission to the course is done on the basis of marks secured in JEE Main followed by the JoSAA/ CSAB counselling procedure.

  • After appearing for the JEE Main exam, candidates have to choose IIIT L as their institution of choice.

Keypoints :-

  • 10% of marks are relaxed for SC/ST category candidates and 5% for OBC candidates.

  • 10% relaxation of marks is also done for PwD candidates and under Defense, category 5% marks are relaxed.

  • DTU B.Tech Application Form can be filled online by visiting the official website.

  • Counseling fee (INR 1400) can be paid through Challan or Net Banking.

  • B.Tech (Evening) admission is made under two categories, i.e., Direct and Sponsored.

  • For B.Tech (Evening) program, the duly filled application form along with the application fee of INR 1000 in the form of DD drawn in favor of ‘Registrar, Delhi Technological University payable at Delhi should reach to Deputy Coordinator (Admin), DTU.

  • 85% of seats are reserved for those candidates who have passed their qualifying examination from the school located in Delhi or NCT of Delhi.

  • 15% of seats are reserved for the candidates belonging to the school located outside Delhi.

  • One seat in each course is reserved for Kashmiri Migrants (KM) and Single Girl Child (SG) who are residing in the NCT of Delhi.

  • One seat over and above the sanctioned intake is reserved for State/ Central Board Toppers.

Scholarships

The Indian Institute of Information Technology, Lucknow offers scholarships to the students. The scholarship is generally provided to the students who belong to the financially weaker sections. This is a way of helping the students who want to gain knowledge and shine in life but are unable to do so because of certain financial constraints. However, the scholarships are generally granted on the basis of merit. To avail the scholarships the candidates are required to maintain a good academic record throughout their educational career.

Apart from these, there are certain scholarship programs which are offered to the students who shine in their fields, even if they do not belong to the financially weaker section of the society.

The Indian Institute of Information Technology Lucknow provides the information to the students about the scholarship programs on the basis of the state and the central scholarship schemes. The Central and the State Government have certain scholarship programs which can be learned about by the students from the office of the Institution. To name one scholarship which is offered to the students at IIIT Lucknow is Arthik Hal, Yuvao Ka Bal. This scholarship has been initiated by the Bihar Government.
