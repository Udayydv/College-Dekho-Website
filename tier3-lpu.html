Lovely Professional University (LPU) is a private university ranked 47th by NIRF in the Indian Universities ranking. LPU has released the LPUNEST 2023 application form for admission to all UG, PG, and PhD including BTech and MBA programs. The last date to apply for LPUNEST 2023 is January 20, 2023. Aspiring candidates can apply online on the official website - admission.lpu.in. The slot booking for online examination will begin on December 26, 2022. Lovely Professional University (LPU) is a Private University situated on the outskirts of Jalandhar, Punjab. The university is approved by University Grants Commission (UGC) and is a member of the Association of Indian Universities (AIU). It is the largest single-campus university in India, with more than 30,000 students, over 4000 faculties and staff, offering 300+ programs across its 45 departments. LPU is also ranked 36th among the Top Universities of India and is ranked 2nd in Business and Economics, 8th in Clinical and Health, 9th in Computer Science and 10th in Engineering & Life Sciences in all India as per the Times Higher Education World University Rankings 2022. LPU ranked 47th in NIRF Universities Rankings 2022.

LPU Placements 2022 broke its own previous record. The highest salary package is offered at INR 3 CPA which is 1.5 times higher as compared to the previous year’s highest package of INR 42 LPA. A total of 8400 Job and Internship offers were made. A student of B.Tech CSE was offered a package of INR 49.4 LPA by Paloalto. Cognizant recruited the maximum number of 670 Students followed by Capgemini which recruited 310 Students. A total of 383 students have been placed with salary packages of INR 10 to INR 62.72 LPA. LPU Placement record proves that its students have become sought-after names amongst top recruiters including Microsoft, Google, Cognizant, Amazon, Capgemini, TCS, and many more. Further, the university has consistently built strong relationships with the industry including the recent tie-ups with Google, SAP, and Oracle, which results in outstanding training opportunities for students. Through LPU’s international exchange programs with renowned institutions around the world, students get exposure to travel and study abroad, at 100% scholarship.

LPU B.Tech Admission 2023 LPU offers B.Tech courses with a wide variety of 20+ specializations. The B.Tech. degree offered for a duration of four years. LPU also offers B.Tech. (Hons) and B.Tech Lateral degree courses. Admission to all these courses is through LPUNEST (LPU National Entrance and Scholarship Test) conducted by the University. LPUNEST will provide a gateway to all engineering programs at LPU and will act as an entrance test for admission to Engineering Programmes at LPU. Candidates with a minimum of 60% aggregate marks in 10+2 or equivalent (with Physics, Mathematics, and English) are eligible for appearing in LPUNEST. LPU B.Tech Eligibility Criteria

  • B.Tech: Passed 12th examination with a minimum aggregate of 60% with Physics, Chemistry, and Maths as compulsory subjects or equivalent.

  • B.Tech Lateral: Pass with 60% aggregate marks in Diploma (Engg.) in any stream or equivalent.
  • B.Tech (Hons.): Pass with 70% aggregate marks in 10+2 (with Physics, Mathematics and English) or equivalent, subject to qualifying LPUNEST.

LPU B.Tech Selection Criteria

  • Admission to B.Tech, B.Tech Lateral and B.Tech (Hons.) is based on LPUNEST conducted by the University.

  • JEE Mains qualified candidates will be exempted from the entrance examination.
  • For students scoring more than or equal to 90% at JEE Mains or 90% aggregate marks in 10+2 are exempted from LPUNEST.
